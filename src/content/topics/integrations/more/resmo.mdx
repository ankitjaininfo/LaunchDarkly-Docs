## Overview

This topic links to Resmo's documentation for its LaunchDarkly integration.

Resmo integrates with LaunchDarkly to enhance the visibility, security, and compliance of your LaunchDarkly resources. Resmo provides insights into your organization’s feature flag management, projects, environments, and team information. 

Additionally, Resmo lets you set up alerts for security violations and changes, supporting notifications through popular communication channels such as Jira, Slack, email, and more.

Resmo manages this integration. To learn how to use it, read Resmo's [LaunchDarkly Integration](https://docs.resmo.com/product/integrations/launchdarkly-integration).
