## Overview

This topic explains how to receive notifications in Microsoft Teams when a feature flag is changed. You can subscribe to notifications for a specific flag.

To use this feature, you must have installed and configured the LaunchDarkly Microsoft Teams app. To learn more, read [Setting up the Microsoft Teams app](/integrations/microsoft-teams/setting-up).

## Subscribing to flag notifications

To receive notifications when a flag is changed, you must follow a flag. To learn more, read [The flags list: Following a flag](/home/organize/flags-list#following-flags).

## Viewing flag change notifications in Microsoft Teams

Flag change notifications will appear in the Microsoft Teams app. Here's how to view them:

1. Log in to Microsoft Teams.
2. Open a chat window with LaunchDarkly.

![Flag notification in Microsoft Teams](teams-ld-flag-notification.png)

- Click the link after "Feature flag:" to open the flag in LaunchDarkly.
- Click **Unfollow changes** to stop receiving notifications for the flag. This will alow unfollow the flag in the LaunchDarkly user interface.
- Click **View flag details** to view more information about the flag.

![Flag details in Microsoft Teams](teams-ld-flag-details.png)
