## Overview

This topic explains LaunchDarkly account plans and the features available on each plan. LaunchDarkly offers three plan tiers to choose from to fit your organization's size and needs.

## Understanding plan features

LaunchDarkly offers Starter, Pro, and Enterprise plans. You can upgrade your organization's plan at any time. Every new account gets a 14-day trial of our Pro features. For more information about plan pricing and feature support, read [Pricing](https://launchdarkly.com/pricing/).
