## LaunchDarkly Customer Knowledge Base

The LaunchDarkly Customer Knowledge Base contains answers to frequently asked questions and troubleshooting instructions. [Access the Customer Knowledge Base in our Support center](https://support.launchdarkly.com/hc/en-us/categories/11335256237851-Customer-Knowledge-Base-).

The LaunchDarkly Academy provides self-guided and live onboarding training. To learn more, visit the [LaunchDarkly Academy](https://academy.launchdarkly.com/).

You can view videos on a variety of topics, from integration demos, to SDK overviews, to a user interface (UI) tutorial [on our YouTube channel](https://www.youtube.com/@LaunchDarkly/videos).
